The game that started it all, Clout World is an open world sandbox game with multiplayer. Clout World was one of the first games from Eade Productions, and although it didn't have the best graphics or gameplay elements, Clout World will always have a special place in our hearts.

ABOUT

Clout World is an open-world sandbox game by Eade Productions. Although basic compared to competition, Clout World is one of Eade Productions' first ever games, and for that it deserves a special place here. The gameplay encourages exploration, creativity, and above all : fun. Although the original Clout World has been lost to time, you can still find earlier versions available for download.

GAMEPLAY

COMBAT

Pure creativity brings pure combat. In Clout World, player vs player combat has been redefined with better damage and hit effects, top notch sound effects, and a great and responsive feel.

EXPLORATION

With a pure, rich, and lively open world, Clout World encourages exploration from the lowest valley to the highest mountain. Find secrets, relax on beaches, or just drive around on the island.

DRIVING

With such a large map, most of the time walking just isn't an option. This is why in Clout World, we think driving isn't just a feature, it's a necessity. Drive around with passengers or solo, on the open road or the wild grass, feel free while driving in Clout World.

BUILDING

Build a house, a road, or a city, in Clout World we think building is one of the most integral parts of a sandbox game. With many prefabs and parts to choose from in our simple but elegant menu, feel free to express your creative desires in a flash.
